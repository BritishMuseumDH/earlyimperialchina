obj1=<b>                    Armour</b><BR><BR>This type of armour is known as lamellar and was based on the types of protection worn in Central Asia. It replaced earlier Chinese <u><font color="#990000"><a href="javascript:openGlos('glossary/c/cord.html','cord')">cord and plaque</a></font></u> armour. <BR><BR> A suit of lamellar armour would be made up of lots of pieces linked together. This would allow the person wearing it to move more easily than if they were wearing a suit of one-piece armour. Some lamellar armour found has been made of iron.
