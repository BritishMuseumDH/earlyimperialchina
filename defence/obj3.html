Mounted archer

Usually this type of archer was used as a scout or skirmisher for the heavy cavalry and was based on the traditional Turkish weapon combination. The use of stirrups made it easier to balance on and control the horse.

Mounted archers were used effectively in A.D. 617 in a battle that helped to end the Sui dynasty and led to the foundation of the Tang dynasty. A detachment of mounted archers and heavy cavalry forced the opposing leader to commit his troops early on in the battle. He was captured and his army defeated.
