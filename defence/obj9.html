Spear

The types of spears used by the soldiers were similar to a halberd. The spearhead had a point and also a hook. Some cavalry men used these types of spears as lances. Spears could also have the traditional pointed tip too.

The spear was a deciding defensive factor at the battle in the Tian Shan mountain range in 99 B.C. The Chinese army had been surrounded by the Ruzhen in between two mountains. The troops formed up so that men with spears and shields formed a protective barrier. Whilst retreating towards Chinese territory the Chinese were ambushed. Temporary spears were made from cart axles. Despite this only 400 Chinese troops escaped from the Ruzhen.
