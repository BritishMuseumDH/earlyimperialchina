Courtyard

Woolley needed a place to dump the rubble from clearing the ziggurat. He had to find an area of the site which had no buildings. To check, he dug two trenches.

In one of these trenches, he found the walls and floor of a building. This was a large courtyard surrounded by rooms. There was also a gate leading towards the ziggurat.

Woolley had to return to England before he could find out what the building was.
