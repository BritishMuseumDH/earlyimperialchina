The Journey to the West, Xi You Ji. - Monkey

This is a famous novel, published in A.D. 1570, recounting the adventures of the Buddhist monk Xuanzang and the monkey Sun Wukong. The monk travelled to India to bring back the Sutras to China. Monkey had to protect him on his journey. Their journey lasted fourteen years. On the way they came up against dragons and many kinds of monsters.
