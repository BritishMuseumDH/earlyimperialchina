Han/Han dynasty

The dynasty that ruled China after the fall of the Qin. They ruled from 206 BC to AD 220. It is divided by historians into two periods. The Western Han is used to describe the period from 206BC to AD9. From AD9 - 23 China was ruled by Wang Mang. In AD25 the period called the Eastern Han started, when the Han dynasty regained power, and this period lasted until AD 220.
